About the Project

AIvoroFI is a decentralized liquidity aggregator and NFT ecosystem built on the Somnia blockchain. Our platform unifies multi-chain DeFi pools, AI-driven yield optimization, and NFT utilities in a single product.

Key Features

Liquidity Aggregator — AI-powered fund management for optimal yield. NFT Collection — Exclusive access to premium DeFi features. NFT Rental System — Users can access benefits without purchasing NFTs. Integration with Partnered NFTs — Expanding the ecosystem and liquidity.

Advantages

AI-powered yield optimization for maximum profits. NFTs with real DeFi utility. Multi-chain access to liquidity pools. No NFT required to use the platform, but holders receive special benefits.

Roadmap

Q2 2025 — Smart contract development & NFT launch. Q3 2025 — Platform testing. Q4 2025 — Public launch.
